
### 2. Grounded Calibration vs Self-Assessment（关键）

**Self-Assessment（自我评估）：**
> AI报告自己的置信度 — "我有60%把握"

**问题：** 不可靠，AI会高估自己

**Grounded Calibration（基于证据的校准）：**
> 需要**确定性证据**，不是来自AI自我报告

核心引用：
> "The solution isn't better prompting or asking the AI to 'be more honest.' The solution is deterministic evidence"
